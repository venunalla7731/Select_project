Preparing to Sell Your Car
There are a few things to do before you start looking for a buyer.

Quick Start

  • Clean your car inside and out
  • Replace burnt-out lights, fuses and top off any fluids
  • Have your mechanic look at it for any needed repairs that could increase its value
  • You may want to get a free Used Car Valuation from Manheim.com
  • Gather any service records you have and make sure the title is ready to transfer to a new owner
